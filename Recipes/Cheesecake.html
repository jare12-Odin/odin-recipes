CHEESECAKE

Description

Want something sweet, savory and simple to make. This cheesecake encompasses all those traits, with only containing 5 ingredients. Make and serve in less than 45 minutes, and have a delicious, fast and savory dessert for you and the whole family.

Ingredients

  • 1 Graham Cracker Pie Crust
  • 21 oz cherry pie filling
  • 16 oz cream cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup condensed milk

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
  2. Beat the cream cheese at room temperature until creamy (1 minute.) Beat in the condensed milk then the eggs until combined.
  3. Pour batter into a pre-made pie crust and bake for 25 minutes. Cool before topping with canned cherry pie filling. Keep refrigerated.

NOTES:

  • Homemade Pie Crust: Combine 9 crushed graham crackers (one sleeve) with 5 Tbsp of melted butter and press into a pie pan.
  • Mini Cheesecakes- Line a muffin pan with cupcake liners. Place 1 tbsp of graham cracker crust (can crumble store-bought if needed) into each liner and press down. Pour 1/4 cup of prepared cheesecake batter into each liner. Bake 15-20 minutes at 350°F.
